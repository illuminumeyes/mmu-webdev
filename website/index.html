About The Marías

The Marías are an American dream pop duo from Los Angeles, consisting of singer María Zardoya and producer/guitarist Zeke Rosenberg. Their ethereal sound combines lush atmospherics, emotional vocals, and hypnotic production to create a unique sonic experience that transports listeners to another dimension.

With critically acclaimed albums and an ever-growing fanbase, The Marías continue to push the boundaries of contemporary pop music while maintaining an intimate, cinematic quality in their work.
